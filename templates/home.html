About Me

As a recent university graduate, I am still exploring and searching for the fields of computer science that I am truly passionate about. Don't get me wrong, I really enjoy building things through programming and want to dedicate myself to the software world. During my four years in college, I completed several projects. Area of Interest

Smart City

Since I was born, I have always lived in the city but never thought about or been involved in city planning, transportation management, or related fields. The only connection I have with the city is my enjoyment of simulation games related to cities, such as City Skylines, Transport Fever 2, and others. I like building my own city, but I always encountered many problems due to poor planning. Recently, both domestic and foreign media reported on the terrible situation of transportation in Taiwan, which made me started to think about how to apply what I have learned to improve city planning and transportation and also considered how I could get into this field.


Thanks to the rapid improvement of computing capabilities, artificial intelligence (AI) has started to emerge and become involved in our everyday lives. These fields, such as automobile, traffic prediction and sensor technologies, create magnificent potential for smart city development. I believe that in the near future, we can not only improve city transportation, but also innovate a completely different lifestyle in cities than what we have today.

Deep Learning

I am amazed by what ChatGPT can accomplish now, and I am also interested in how it was built and what the fundamentals of this LLM are. Additionally, I am interested in Graph Neural Networks (GNN), because I believe most real-world problems can be turned into graphs. If we can use deep learning to properly solve graph-related problems, as we did with image recognition and natural language processing (NLP), it could be a game changer.
