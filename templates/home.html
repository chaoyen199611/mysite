About Me

As a recent university graduate, I am still exploring and searching for the fields of computer science that I am truly passionate about. Don't get me wrong, I really enjoy building things through programming and want to dedicate myself to the software world. During my four years in college, I completed several projects. One such project was developing a basic smart plant care system with Arduino.

I want to study or work abroad in the future. Doing so will help me become a global citizen with a global mindset, ready to overcome different challenges. Although I have been living in Taiwan since I was born, I don’t want to limit my life experiences to this country. I aspire to explore the world and push myself to new heights.
